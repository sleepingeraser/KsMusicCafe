Reservations

Please take note of the following below:

  1. Please note that reservations will only be held for 15 minutes before being released and a dining session time of 2 hours applies for all tables, which begins from allotted booking commencement time.
  2. If you can't find your preferred booking time, please call our concierge team at 4239 5011 - they will be able to assist you with the closest alternative.
  3. Do note that we keep an average of 30% of the venue's tables for walk-in customers, all walk-ins will be attended to on a first- come, first-served basis.
