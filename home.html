About Us

KS Café Studio is not just a place to listen music, play in a studio room and enjoy hot beverages and desserts. Since music can improve mood, decrease pain and anxiety, we partnered with Yamaha by providing us all the instruments they have. By visiting our café, you're not only able to relax yourself but also enter a world full of music to escape from reality and going home early.
